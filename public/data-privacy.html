Data Privacy

1. Introduction

Welcome to our Data Privacy Policy. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our services.

2. Information We Collect

When you sign up for our services, we collect the following information:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your encrypted password
  • Your IP address
  • Date and time of last login

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your account
  • To authenticate your access to our services
  • To enhance the security of our platform

4. Data Encryption and Storage

Your notes are encrypted on your device before being sent to our servers. We use industry-standard encryption techniques to ensure the security of your data during transmission and storage. Each user's encryption key is unique and securely stored.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties, except as necessary to provide our services or comply with legal obligations.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. You can also request information about the data we hold about you and request that we restrict the processing of your data.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Data Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we encourage you to review the policy periodically for updates.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our Data Privacy Policy or our practices regarding your personal information, please contact us at privacy@focushub.cloud.
